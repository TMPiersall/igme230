New Media Logo

Motion Package

  • Fall 2018
  • Adobe Illustrator/After Effects

Description

I created a new title sequence and additional animated assets for use in NMD's social media and promotional work.
The goal I set for myself was to try and embody the spirit of New Media Design. To get this across I chose a sketching and wireframing theme, as within the major we all use these skills often.

Process Work

Sketches

I started by narrowing down what it means to be in New Media and what would then make the most sense as a channel ID. I went through many options as we are versitile and can do many things.

Styleframes

In Adobe Illustrator I hashed out some styleframes of key moments of the animated opening. It would start by drawing in the New Media logo to look like it was a wireframe. The intro would then end by the true logo popping in and gleaming like the screens we design for.

The Complete Package

To finish off I created additional components to the intro animation, namely a transition slide and an end card. Throughout the animation process I used namely Adobe After Effects as well as Adobe Illustrator for certain componnents and masking.
