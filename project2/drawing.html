Jena in Watercolor

Watercolor Painting

  • Spring 2018
  • Watercolors

Description

Watercolor has always been a favorite medium of mine. I enjoy guiding the paint into a picture. This piece of my dog at home was particularly difficult since she is mostly a white dog, so I almost had to paint around her form. I left the background unfinished so that the viewer could better appreciate the simlicity of the focus rather than be distracted by the flowers that were originally in the reference photo.
