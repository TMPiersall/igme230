Logo Design

Rebrand for Local Tea Company

  • Fall 2018
  • Adobe Illustrator

Description

This is still a Work in Progress. Our branding team is hard at work trying to create a brand for ArrAyeTea, a pirate themed tea company. The work you're seeing here is my contribution to the goal of making a finalized brand. So far all work has been done in Adobe Illustrator.

Process Work

Sketches and Styleboards


We each were given a focus for our ideas. Mine was to combine modern design with the concept of traditional pirate. I had to find a cohesive look for the colors and the logo design.

Vector Iterations

I began to create black and white vector versions of my sketched logos to see how they looked digitally and we've been meeting regularly to discuss progress.
