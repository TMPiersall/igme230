RIT News Website Redesign

  • Spring 2018
  • Adobe Illustrator/After Effects

Description

RIT has a monthy magazine for students to enjoy. The goal was to redesign their website so that it's more accessable to students and faculty who read and enjoy podcasts
I wanted to make it more customizable to make it easier for returning users to access content that they most enjoy or read most often. I wanted to accomplish this goal without turning away users who have no desire to create an account so I focused on a customizable but not pushy layout.

Process Work

Wireframes


To get a better idea of what I wanted the site to do and look like I put together a series of wireframes that laid out the basic idea of functionality and layout.

Comps

The wireframes led to some color compositions to get a better feel for style and effectiveness of interface elements. Ultimately I decided the color choice and icons were just not working and would have to be simplified.

Finals


For the final product, I followed up with mobile components and additional screens to demonstrate the ideas of potential interactivity as well as the main desktop page seen up top.
